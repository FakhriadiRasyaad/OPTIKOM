Study Group

Optical Communication System Study Group is a learning forum for students to explore optical communication concepts and applications. The study group also helps students exchange ideas and prepare for exams and assignments, with the aim of strengthening theoretical understanding and practical skills.

Company Visit

This activity is an opportunity to see the systems and network infrastructure used by the Telecommunications Industry today as well as an experience to increase a better understanding in the field of telecommunications.

Optical Fiber Training

Optical Fiber Training Opticomm Laboratory is a training program organized to provide an in-depth understanding of fiber optic technology. This training is designed for students, researchers, and practitioners who want to develop their expertise in the installation, testing, and maintenance of fiber optic networks. Through practical and theoretical sessions, participants will learn the basic principles of fiber optics, splicing techniques, measurements using OTDR, as well as fiber optic applications in the telecommunications and information technology industries. The program aims to equip participants with skills that are relevant and ready to be applied in the world of work.

Research

Research at the Opticomm Laboratory focuses on development and innovation in the field of optical communications, with special attention to fiber optic technology, wireless optics, and high-speed data communication networks. The laboratory conducts a wide range of research, from the design and optimization of optical components, optical communication systems, to the application of photonic technology in telecommunications and computer networks. In addition to basic research, Opticomm Laboratory is also active in applying optical technology in industrial solutions and modern telecommunications infrastructure. Through collaborations with academic institutions and industries, the laboratory continues to contribute in advancing the science and technology of optical communication.

Farawell Party

Farewell Lab OCL is an event as a form of appreciation and respect for alumni who have completed graduation as well as a reunion and appreciation for their contributions during their work in the laboratory. In addition, this activity is a moment for alumni to share experiences, impressions, and provide motivation for active members in developing academic and professional careers in the field of optical communication.

Competition

Opticomm Laboratory Competition, such as PKM (Student Creativity Program) and Innovillage, is a competition that aims to encourage students within Opticomm Laboratory to develop innovative ideas in the field of optical communication and related technologies. The competition challenges participants to create creative solutions that are relevant to the needs of society, both in the industrial and social sectors. In addition, this competition is also a means for assistants to apply the knowledge they learn in real projects, promote teamwork, and improve research and presentation skills. Through this competition, it is hoped that new innovations that have a positive impact will emerge, as well as foster the spirit of entrepreneurship and research among students.
